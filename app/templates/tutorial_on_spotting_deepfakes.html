How to Spot Deepfakes - A Quick Tutorial

Spotting deepfakes can be challenging, but here are some tips to help you identify them:

  1. Check Facial Inconsistencies: Pay attention to unnatural facial expressions, blinking, or lip-syncing that may appear inconsistent with the rest of the video.
  2. Examine Lighting and Shadows: Analyze the lighting and shadows in the video. In some deepfakes, lighting may not match the surrounding environment, leading to inconsistencies.
  3. Look for Unnatural Artifacts: Deepfakes may exhibit artifacts or distortions, especially around the face. Zoom in on the video to inspect for any unnatural pixelation or blurring.
  4. Verify the Source: Consider the source of the content. If the video is from an unverified or suspicious source, be cautious about its authenticity.

By staying vigilant and applying these techniques, you can enhance your ability to identify potential deepfakes.
